The Team

Group 9 - CWK Studios are founded by Craig Reade, Will Gajewski and Kevin Go. The team had created the U.F.O. Sighting web-based tower defense game. You can scroll down to learn about each team member.

Craig Reade

Craig Reade is a Senior, and is scheduled to graduate after the Spring 2023 semester. He is currently working as a Technical Support Specialist in Southern California. When he isn't spending time with his family, he enjoys camping, reading, and is active in his local Masonic lodge.

Will Gajewski

Will Gajewski will be graduating from UIS this Fall 2022. He has held positions as a back-end Java developer and an AEM Developer . He’s currently working as an AEM Developer at Merkle - Dentsu. His hobbies include rock climbing, camping, reading, and D&D.

Kevin Go

Kevin Go will be graduating from UIS this Fall 2022. He had held positions as a Software Engineer, Technical Project Manager and Scrum Master. He’s currently working as a Sales Development Representative at SoftwareOne. He enjoys biking, watching movies, tattooing and building things!
