How To Play



  • Legal positions are highlighted with a white circle.
  • Each turret costs 50 gold.
  • The first turret upgrade costs 100 gold.
  • The second turret upgrade costs 200 gold.
  • You will earn 10 gold for each enemy destroyed.
  • Enemies will travel along the road.
  • You have 10 hearts. Each enemy that enters the city costs you 1 heart.
  • If you need to pause, navigate to another tab in your browser. The game will only run if the tab is active.
  • If you run out of hearts, your game is over. Can you beat your high score?

Invasion!


U.F.O.s have been sighted over the countryside! Their intentions are clear - you must stop them before they reach our cities. Fortify the surrounding area with weapons, and stop the hostile alien advance.

Your people are counting on you!





The white dots near the roads are legal positions to build a defensive turret. Each turret costs 50 gold, and you will earn 10 gold for each U.F.O. you destroy. Place wisely!

Enemies will travel along the road. Fortunately, most enemy saucers react poorly to water, and have difficulty passing over natural terrain.

These aliens are crafty and learn from their mistakes. Each wave will be stronger than the last. If 10 saucers make their way into the city, all hope (and your game) is lost!

Each placed turret can be upgraded twice - the first upgrade costs 100 gold, the second costs 200 gold. Upgraded turrets fire faster, fire multiple, stronger projectiles, and have a longer range! A reminder of upgrade cost can be found beneath the game board.

Playing on a smaller screen? Use the Zoom function in your browser window to make the board a little smaller. CTRL/CMD and + or - will zoom in and out on most browsers. Sorry - U.F.O. Sighting isn't compatible with mobile browsers at this time!
